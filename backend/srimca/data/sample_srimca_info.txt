SRIMCA full name is Shrimad Rajchandra Institute of Management and Computer Application.
SRIMCA is located at Maliba Campus, Gopal Vidyanagar, Bardoli, Surat, Gujarat.
SRIMCA is a constituent college of Uka Tarsadia University.
SRIMCA offers MCA and MBA programmes approved by AICTE.
MCA programme started in the year 2002.
MBA programme started in the year 2004.
MCA 2nd Semester commenced from Thursday, 30 January 2025.
MCA 4th Semester commenced from Monday, 16 December 2024.
BCA 4th and 6th Semester commenced from Monday, 16 December 2024.
SRIMCA mission is to remain on the cutting edge of education, research and service to industry and society.
SRIMCA vision is to become a globally recognized premier educational institute for academic excellence.
SRIMCA has more than 250 computers with Wi-Fi connectivity.
SRIMCA provides 200 MBps dedicated internet leased line.
SRIMCA library contains national and international books and journals.
SRIMCA has an auditorium, seminar hall and modern classrooms.
